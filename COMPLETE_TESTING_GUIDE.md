### **FLOW 4: Test Defense Length Impact**

The evaluation considers **defense length** as a quality signal:

**Short Defense (<50 chars):**
```
"This choice works because it's better."
```
→ **Frost** - Too shallow, no causal explanation

**Medium Defense (50-150 chars):**
```
"By diversifying our clients, we reduce the risk of losing 
all revenue if one client leaves. This creates more stability."
```
→ **Warning** - Good start, but missing second-order effects

**Long Defense (150+ chars):**
```
"By diversifying our client portfolio, we break the positive 
feedback loop that makes us dependent. This creates organizational 
resilience. When one client leaves, we still have revenue. This also 
gives us negotiating power with any single client."
```
→ **Ignition** - Deep causal reasoning

**Edge Case:** Even with wrong button, long defense can improve score!
